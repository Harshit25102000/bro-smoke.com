About Bro-Smoke

Bro-smoke is developed with primary goal of helping my smoker friends in quitting
Initially developed in my leisure and as a remedy for my boredom I believe With Bro-smoke you can easily and instantly add your cigarette consumption and then keep a track and check your consumption. You can look at your monthly , weekly and day wise stats and these numbers will help you realise how much you smoke and how much you spend on it. With Bro-Smoke you can also look at your friend's status and check on him too.
It's still under development and need your feedback to improve.
Hope it helps you to track and quit smoking.
